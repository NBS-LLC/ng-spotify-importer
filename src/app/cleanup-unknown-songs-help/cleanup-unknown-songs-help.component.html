Help - Cleanup Unknown Songs

Cleanup Unknown Songs is an experimental feature. When enabled it removes special characters from unknown song titles and artist names.

The idea being that the "cleaned up" songs will then be found (known).

The following cleanup occurs on each unknown song:

  • the parenthesis portion of the song title is removed
  • the ampersand (&) portion from song title and artist name is removed
  • single quotes (') from the title and artist name are removed
  • the "featuring" portion of the song title is removed
